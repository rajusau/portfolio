Axis Bank Stock Price Analysis (2000 - 2020)

PYTHON (Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)

About the Project

This project analyzes Axis Bank's stock market data from 2000 to 2020, focusing on trading volumes, price trends, and key metrics like VWAP. It aims to uncover trading patterns and assess market performance to identify potential buying opportunities. The analysis utilizes data from Kaggle's Nifty50 dataset, providing insights into stock behavior, liquidity, and volatility. By evaluating historical price movements and trading volumes, the project offers valuable information for making informed investment decisions.
