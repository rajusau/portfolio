Pizza Sales Analysis

MySQL

About the Project

This project involves analyzing pizza sales data using SQL. The database `pizzahut` was created using data imported from four CSV files. The analysis covers various aspects of pizza sales, including total revenue, most popular pizzas, and revenue distribution.

GitHub Repository

Percentage Contribution of Pizza Categories

Contribution of each pizza category to total revenue.

Analyzed the revenue share of each pizza category, revealing which categories contribute most to overall revenue.

Cumulative Revenue Over Time

Tracking revenue growth over various periods.

Calculated cumulative revenue over time to identify trends and patterns in sales growth or decline.

Top 3 Pizza Types by Revenue

Best-selling pizzas based on revenue generated.

Identified the top three pizza types contributing the highest revenue, helping to focus on most profitable items.

Revenue by Pizza Category Breakdown

Revenue distribution across different pizza categories.

Provided a detailed breakdown of revenue by each pizza category to understand which categories perform best financially.

Hourly Order Distribution Analysis

Order volume trends by hour of day.

Analyzed order distribution by hour to determine peak times and optimize staffing and promotions.

Daily Average Pizza Orders

Average number of pizzas ordered per day.

Calculated the average daily pizza orders to assess overall demand and identify trends in customer behavior.
